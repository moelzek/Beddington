# Beddington soothe assets

These are local 15-minute MP3 audition assets for the soothe dashboard. The
dashboard reads [`catalog.json`](catalog.json) for each preset's one-word label,
group (`sounds` or `music`), feel, suggested use, and avoid note.

`chime.wav` is separate: it is the short wake acknowledgement sound for the
voice assistant and is not offered as a soothe preset.

Keep first playback tests at low volume on a laptop before trying the Pi speaker
or amplifier. These assets are for local auditioning and preset testing; they
are not evidence that any sound will settle a baby. The womb-like asset is kept
for research/audition use only and must not be framed as medical, safety, or
wellbeing reassurance.
